# Product Requirements Document (PRD) Draft

Use this template when you want Claude to help you turn rough product thinking into a complete PRD.

This is a user-facing intake template:
- fill in as much or as little as you already know
- leave unknown sections blank rather than guessing
- include links to related Jira tickets, epics, docs, or examples when they are helpful

The final PRD should keep the structure below and be suitable for Confluence-compatible markdown.
